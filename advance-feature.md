You are tasked with upgrading an existing Point-of-Sale (POS) system into a fully advanced, enterprise-grade, cloud-native commerce platform.

IMPORTANT:
- Do NOT include basic POS features (e.g., simple checkout, manual inventory, basic receipts).
- Focus ONLY on advanced, scalable, automated, and intelligent capabilities.
- The system must function as a central business operating system, not just a transaction tool.

CORE OBJECTIVE:
Transform the POS into a unified platform that integrates sales, operations, customer intelligence, workforce management, and AI-driven automation.

---

### 1. CLOUD & ARCHITECTURE
- Convert system into cloud-native (multi-tenant SaaS architecture)
- Real-time synchronization across multiple branches/devices
- Offline-first capability with automatic conflict resolution
- API-first architecture for extensibility
- Role-based access control (RBAC) across all modules

---

### 2. SMART INVENTORY SYSTEM
- Real-time multi-location inventory tracking
- Predictive stock forecasting using historical sales data
- Automated purchase order generation based on thresholds
- Batch tracking, expiry management, and supplier mapping
- Inventory movement logs + audit trails

---

### 3. OMNICHANNEL SALES ENGINE
- Unified order management (in-store, online, social platforms)
- Integration-ready with eCommerce, Facebook, and delivery platforms
- Centralized product catalog with dynamic pricing
- Click-and-collect and delivery orchestration logic

---

### 4. CUSTOMER INTELLIGENCE (CRM)
- Build full customer profiles with behavioral tracking
- Implement segmentation engine (VIP, repeat, churn risk)
- Loyalty system with programmable rewards logic
- Automated engagement triggers (SMS, email, push)

---

### 5. ADVANCED ANALYTICS & BI
- Real-time dashboards with drill-down capabilities
- Product performance analysis (top sellers, dead stock, margin)
- Sales trend analysis and forecasting
- Staff productivity and transaction analytics
- Exportable reports + data warehouse readiness

---

### 6. AI & AUTOMATION LAYER
- AI-powered upsell and cross-sell recommendations at checkout
- Demand forecasting models
- Anomaly detection (fraud, unusual sales patterns)
- Dynamic pricing engine based on demand, time, and inventory
- Automated business insights and alerts

---

### 7. PAYMENT & FINTECH INTEGRATION
- Support multi-payment methods (cards, eWallets, QR, BNPL)
- Real-time payment reconciliation
- Integration with accounting systems (e.g., QuickBooks, Xero)
- Automated tax/VAT computation and reporting

---

### 8. WORKFORCE MANAGEMENT
- Staff scheduling and shift planning
- Time tracking with clock-in/out system
- Commission and incentive tracking
- Role-based dashboards (cashier, manager, owner)

---

### 9. HARDWARE & DEVICE FLEXIBILITY
- Cross-platform support (desktop, tablet, mobile, kiosk)
- Peripheral integration (barcode scanners, printers, cash drawers)
- Offline mode with seamless sync

---

### 10. SECURITY & COMPLIANCE
- End-to-end encryption
- Full audit logs for all transactions and actions
- Compliance-ready (PCI-DSS for payments)
- Secure authentication (MFA, OAuth)

---

### 11. INTEGRATION ECOSYSTEM
- Open API marketplace for third-party integrations
- Webhooks for real-time external system updates
- Integration with logistics, marketing tools, ERP systems

---

### 12. AUTOMATION & WORKFLOW ENGINE
- Rule-based automation (e.g., low stock → auto reorder)
- Custom triggers and actions across modules
- Business workflow builder (no-code/low-code logic)

---

### OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
- Provide system architecture diagram (text-based or structured)
- Define database schema (optimized for scalability)
- Outline core services/modules (microservices if applicable)
- Include API structure (REST/GraphQL)
- Suggest tech stack (frontend, backend, infra)
- Include scalability considerations (10 → 10,000+ users)

---

### CONTEXT ALIGNMENT (CRITICAL):
- Design the system to be extensible for service-based businesses (e.g., salons, repair services, home services)
- Ensure compatibility with booking systems, job dispatching, and service tracking
- Prioritize automation, intelligence, and operational efficiency

---

FINAL GOAL:
Deliver a future-proof Advanced POS System that acts as a Business Operating System capable of scaling across industries and integrating with service marketplaces like LocalPro.
