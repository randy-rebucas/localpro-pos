# LocalPro POS — The All-in-One Cloud Point of Sale System for Modern Businesses

Are you running a retail shop, restaurant, salon, or service-based business? Meet **LocalPro POS** — a powerful, cloud-based Point of Sale system built to help you manage everything from sales to scheduling, inventory to invoicing — all from one platform.

Whether you're a solo entrepreneur or managing multiple branches, LocalPro POS scales with your business.

---

## SALES & CHECKOUT

- Fast, intuitive POS terminal with product grid and search
- Pin your best-selling products for one-tap access
- Support for **Cash, Card, and Digital/e-Wallet** payments
- Automatic change calculation for cash transactions
- Apply discount and promo codes at checkout
- Save and park carts — resume transactions anytime
- Custom receipt generation with your branding, logo, and contact info
- Unique receipt numbers for every transaction
- Full and partial refund processing

## PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

- Create regular products, service items, and product bundles
- Product variations (size, color, type) with individual pricing and stock
- SKU tracking, product images, and categories
- Bundle multiple items at a custom price — perfect for combos and packages
- Bundle performance analytics to see what sells

## INVENTORY & STOCK CONTROL

- Real-time stock tracking with live updates
- Automatic stock deduction on sale and restoration on refund
- Manual stock adjustments, refills, and transfers
- Branch-specific stock levels for multi-location businesses
- Variation-specific inventory tracking
- Low stock alerts with configurable thresholds
- Complete stock movement history — every change tracked with who, when, and why

## BOOKING & APPOINTMENT SCHEDULING

- Create and manage service appointments with customer details
- Assign staff to bookings with automatic conflict detection

- Calendar views: month, week, and day
- Booking statuses: Pending, Confirmed, Completed, Cancelled, No-Show
- Automated email and SMS reminders before appointments
- Batch reminder sending for upcoming bookings

## MULTI-BRANCH SUPPORT

- Manage multiple store locations from a single dashboard

- Centralized reporting across all locations
