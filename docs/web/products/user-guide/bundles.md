# Product Bundles - User Guide

Complete guide to creating and managing product bundles in LocalPro POS.

## Overview

Product bundles allow you to group multiple products or services together and sell them as a single item, often at a discounted price.

## When to Use Bundles

Use bundles for:
- Product packages (e.g., "Starter Kit")
- Service packages (e.g., "Haircut + Styling")
- Promotional offers (e.g., "Buy 2 Get 1 Free")
- Gift sets
- Combo meals

## Creating a Bundle

### Step 1: Access Bundle Creation

1. Go to **Admin** → **Bundles**
2. Click **"Add Bundle"** button
3. Bundle creation form appears

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of bundles page with Add Bundle button]

### Step 2: Enter Bundle Information

Fill in bundle details:

#### Required Fields

- **Bundle Name** - Name of the bundle
- **Price** - Bundle selling price
- **Items** - Products/services in bundle

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of bundle form]

#### Optional Fields

- **Description** - Bundle description
- **SKU** - Bundle SKU (auto-generated if blank)
- **Category** - Bundle category
- **Image** - Bundle image

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot showing optional fields]

### Step 3: Add Bundle Items

1. Click **"Add Item"** button
2. Search and select product
3. Enter quantity for that product
4. Repeat for all items
5. Items appear in list

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of adding bundle items]

### Step 4: Configure Bundle Settings

#### Stock Tracking

- **Track Bundle Stock** - Track bundle as single unit
- **Track Individual Items** - Track each item separately
- Choose based on your needs

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of stock tracking options]

#### Pricing

- **Bundle Price** - Total price for bundle
- **Individual Prices** - Sum of individual item prices
- **Savings** - Difference shown automatically

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of pricing display]

### Step 5: Save Bundle

1. Review all information
2. Check items and quantities
3. Verify price
4. Click **"Save Bundle"**
5. Bundle created

## Bundle Stock Management

### Stock Tracking Options

#### Option 1: Track Bundle Stock

- Bundle treated as single product
- Stock tracked for bundle only
- Individual items not tracked separately
- Use for pre-packaged bundles

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of bundle stock tracking]

#### Option 2: Track Individual Items

- Each item in bundle tracked separately
- Stock deducted from individual items
- More accurate inventory
- Use for custom bundles

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of individual item tracking]

### Stock Updates

When bundle is sold:
- If tracking bundle: Bundle stock decreases
- If tracking items: Each item's stock decreases
- Stock movements recorded
- Low stock alerts work normally

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Bundle Not Appearing in POS

**Problem**: Bundle doesn't show in POS

**Solution**:
- Check bundle is active
- Verify bundle has items
- Check stock availability
- Refresh POS page
