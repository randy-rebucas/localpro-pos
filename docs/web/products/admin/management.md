# Product Management - Admin Guide

Complete administrative guide for managing products in LocalPro POS.

## Overview

This guide covers all administrative functions for product management, including bulk operations, advanced settings, and product configuration.

## Accessing Product Management

1. Log in as Admin or Manager
2. Navigate to **Admin** → **Products**
3. Product management interface loads

## Advanced Product Settings

### Product Pinning

Pin important products to top of POS:

1. Edit product
2. Enable "Pin to Top" option
3. Save
4. Product appears first in POS

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of pinning option]

### Product Visibility

Control where product appears:

- **POS Only** - Visible in POS, not in public catalog
- **Catalog Only** - Visible in catalog, not in POS
- **Both** - Visible everywhere
- **Hidden** - Not visible anywhere

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of visibility settings]

### Low Stock Alerts

Configure stock alerts:

1. Edit product
2. Go to "Stock Settings"
3. Set "Low Stock Threshold"
4. Enable alerts
5. Save
