# Reports & Analytics

Complete guide to reports and analytics in LocalPro POS.

## Overview

The Reports module provides comprehensive business analytics, including sales reports, product performance, profit & loss statements, and more.

## Features

- ✅ Sales reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
- ✅ Product performance reports
- ✅ Profit & Loss statements
- ✅ Cash drawer reports
- ✅ VAT/Tax reports
- ✅ Custom date ranges
- ✅ Interactive charts
- ✅ Export capabilities

## Quick Start

### Viewing Sales Report

1. Go to **Reports**
2. Click **"Sales Report"**
3. Select date range
4. View report
