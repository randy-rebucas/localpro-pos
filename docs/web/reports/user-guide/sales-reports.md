# Sales Reports - User Guide

Complete guide to viewing and analyzing sales reports in 1POS.

## Overview

Sales reports provide detailed insights into your business performance, including revenue, transaction trends, and revenue by product or category.

## Accessing Reports

### From Main Menu

1. Navigate to **Reports**
2. Sales reports section appears
3. Select report type

## Reading Sales Reports

### Understanding Totals

**Total Sales**: Sum of all completed transactions
**Total Transactions**: Number of transactions
**Average Transaction**: Total Sales ÷ Transactions
**Refunds**: Total refunded amount
**Net Sales**: Total Sales - Refunds

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot explaining totals]

### Understanding Trends

- **Upward Trend** - Sales increasing
- **Downward Trend** - Sales decreasing
- **Stable Trend** - Consistent sales
- **Seasonal Patterns** - Regular fluctuations
