# Payment Methods - User Guide

Complete guide to processing different payment methods in LocalPro POS.

## Overview

LocalPro POS supports three payment methods:
- **Cash** - Physical cash payments
- **Card** - Credit/debit card payments
- **Digital** - Digital wallet payments (PayPal, Venmo, etc.)

## Payment Method Selection

### Accessing Payment Options

1. Add products to cart
2. Review cart totals
3. Click **"Process Payment"** button
4. Payment method selection appears

## Cash Payments

### When to Use

- Customer pays with physical cash
- Quick transactions
- Small amounts
- No card processing needed

### Processing Cash Payment

#### Step 1: Select Cash

1. Click **"Cash"** button
2. Cash payment dialog appears
3. Shows total amount

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of cash payment dialog]

#### Step 2: Enter Amount Received

1. Enter amount customer gives you
2. Use number pad or type amount
3. Amount formats automatically

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of amount input]

#### Step 3: Verify Change

1. System calculates change automatically
2. Change amount displays
3. Verify change is correct

**Example**:
- Total: $23.84
- Cash Received: $30.00
- Change: $6.16

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot showing change calculation]

#### Step 4: Complete Payment

1. Review all amounts
2. Click **"Complete Payment"**
3. Transaction processed
4. Receipt generated

> **Screenshot Placeholder**: [Add screenshot of cash payment completion]

### Cash Payment Tips

- ✅ Count cash received carefully
- ✅ Verify change calculation
- ✅ Count change before giving to customer
- ✅ Keep cash drawer organized
- ✅ Record large bills separately

### Handling Exact Change

If customer pays exact amount:
1. Enter exact total amount
2. Change shows as $0.00
3. Complete payment

### Handling Insufficient Cash
