# Inventory Management

Complete guide to managing inventory and stock in 1POS.

## Overview

The Inventory Management system provides comprehensive stock tracking, real-time updates, low stock alerts, and complete stock movement history.

## Features

- ✅ Real-time stock tracking
- ✅ Automatic stock updates on sales/refunds
- ✅ Low stock alerts
- ✅ Stock movement history
- ✅ Multi-branch stock tracking
- ✅ Stock adjustments
- ✅ Stock refills
- ✅ Inventory reports

## Quick Start

### Viewing Stock Levels

1. Go to **Inventory** page
2. View all products with stock levels
3. Filter by category or search
4. See stock status (In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock)

### Updating Stock

1. Go to product page
2. Click **"Refill Stock"** or **"Adjust Stock"**
3. Enter quantity
4. Add reason
5. Save
