# Low Stock Alerts - User Guide

Complete guide to setting up and managing low stock alerts in 1POS.

## Overview

Low stock alerts notify you when product inventory falls below a specified threshold, helping you maintain adequate stock levels and avoid stockouts.

## Understanding Low Stock

### What is Low Stock?

Low stock occurs when:
- Product quantity falls below threshold
- Restocking is needed soon
- Risk of stockout exists

### Stock Status Levels

- **In Stock** (Green) - Above threshold
- **Low Stock** (Yellow) - Below threshold
- **Out of Stock** (Red) - Zero or negative

### Per Product Threshold

1. Go to product edit page
2. Scroll to "Stock Settings"
3. Enter "Low Stock Threshold"
4. Save settings
