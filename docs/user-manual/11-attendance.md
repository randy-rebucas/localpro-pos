# 11. Attendance & Timekeeping

**Available to:** All Users (clock in/out) | Manager+ (view all staff)

## Clocking In

1. The **Attendance Clock** widget appears on the dashboard or in the sidebar
2. Click **Clock In**
3. Your clock-in time is recorded
4. The timer starts showing your working hours

## Taking a Break

1. Click **Start Break**
2. The system records break start time
3. When returning, click **End Break**
4. Break duration is tracked separately from working hours

## Clocking Out

1. Click **Clock Out**
2. Your session summary shows:
   - Clock-in time
   - Clock-out time
   - Total hours worked
   - Break duration
   - Net working hours

## Viewing Your Attendance

1. Navigate to **Profile** or **Attendance**
2. View your attendance history:
   - Date
   - Clock in time
   - Clock out time
   - Break duration
   - Total hours
   - Status (On Time, Late, Absent)

## Manager View

**Available to:** Manager, Admin, Owner

1. Navigate to **Admin > Attendance**
2. View all staff attendance:
   - Filter by employee, date range, or status
   - See who is currently clocked in
   - View tardiness patterns
   - Export attendance data

### Attendance Summary

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Present** | Staff who clocked in today |
| **Late** | Staff who clocked in after expected time |
| **Absent** | Staff who did not clock in |
| **On Break** | Staff currently on break |
| **Total Hours** | Combined working hours for the period |

## Attendance Notifications

If configured in **Settings > Notifications**:

| Notification | Recipient | Trigger |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| **Late Arrival** | Manager | Staff clocks in after scheduled time |
| **Missed Clock-Out** | Staff + Manager | Staff hasn't clocked out by end of day |
| **Attendance Violation** | Manager | Repeated tardiness or absences |
| **Break Exceeded** | Staff | Break duration exceeds allowed time |

## Auto Clock-Out

The system can automatically clock out staff who forget:
- **Default:** 12 hours after clock-in (configurable)
- The auto clock-out is flagged in the attendance record
- A notification is sent to the staff member and their manager
- Configure in **Settings > Automations > Auto Clock-Out**

## Attendance Reports

1. Navigate to **Reports** or **Admin > Attendance**
2. Select date range
3. Export as CSV, Excel, or PDF
4. Report includes: hours per employee, tardiness, breaks, overtime

## Attendance Trends

The **Attendance Trends Chart** provides visual insights:
- Daily attendance patterns
- Average clock-in times
- Break duration trends
- Compare periods (this week vs. last week)
