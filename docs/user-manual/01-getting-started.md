# 1. Getting Started

## System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
- Stable internet connection (offline mode available for POS)
- For mobile: iOS 16.4+ or Android with Chrome

## Accessing 1POS

1. Open your browser and navigate to your store URL:
   - Format: `https://yourstorename.1pos.app` or `https://1pos.app/yourstorename`
2. You will be redirected to the login page

## Logging In

1POS supports three login methods:

### Email & Password Login

1. Enter your registered **email address**
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

### PIN Login

1. Click **PIN Login** on the login screen
2. Enter your **4-6 digit PIN**
3. The system authenticates you immediately
4. Best for quick cashier shift changes

### QR Code Login

1. Click **QR Login** on the login screen
2. The system displays a QR scanner
3. Present your unique QR code badge
4. The system reads the code and logs you in instantly
5. Ideal for staff with printed QR badges

## First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

1. Your Admin or Owner will provide your initial credentials
2. Navigate to **Profile** from the top-right menu
3. Click **Change Password**
4. Enter your current password, then set a new one
5. Click **Save**

## Navigation Overview

After login, you will see:

- **Top Navigation Bar** — Shows your store name, current user, language selector, and logout
- **Sidebar / Menu** — Lists all modules available to your role
- **Main Content Area** — Displays the active page

## Language Selection

1POS supports multiple languages. To change your language:

1. Click the **language icon** in the top navigation bar
2. Select your preferred language (English, Spanish, etc.)
3. The interface updates immediately

## Installing 1POS as an App (PWA)

1POS can be installed on your device for an app-like experience:

### On Desktop (Chrome)
1. Look for the **install icon** in the browser address bar
2. Click **Install**
3. 1POS opens as a standalone window

### On Android
1. Open 1POS in Chrome
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** > **Add to Home Screen**
3. Tap **Install**

### On iOS
1. Open 1POS in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**

## Logging Out

1. Click your **profile icon** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Logout**
3. Your session is terminated and tokens are revoked

> **Security Note:** Always log out when leaving a shared terminal. Sessions expire automatically after inactivity, but manual logout is recommended.
