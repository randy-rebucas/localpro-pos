# 14. Subscriptions & Billing

**Available to:** Owner only

## Overview

1POS uses a subscription model. Your plan determines available features, user limits, and transaction caps.

## Viewing Your Subscription

1. Navigate to **Subscription** from the sidebar
2. View your current plan:
   - Plan name and tier
   - Billing cycle (monthly/annual)
   - Next billing date
   - Current usage vs. limits
   - Features included

## Subscription Plans

| Feature | Free | Starter | Professional | Enterprise |
|---------|------|---------|-------------|------------|
| **Users** | 2 | 5 | 15 | Unlimited |
| **Products** | 50 | 200 | 1,000 | Unlimited |
| **Transactions/month** | 100 | 500 | 5,000 | Unlimited |
| **Branches** | 1 | 1 | 3 | Unlimited |
| **Reports** | Basic | Standard | Advanced | Full |
| **Bookings** | - | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Multi-currency** | - | - | Yes | Yes |
| **Custom branding** | - | - | Yes | Yes |
| **API access** | - | - | Yes | Yes |
| **Priority support** | - | - | - | Yes |

> Plans and limits may vary. Check your **Subscription** page for exact details.

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Navigate to **Subscription**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select the desired plan
4. Review pricing and feature comparison
5. Click **Subscribe**
6. Complete payment via PayPal
7. New features are available immediately

## Downgrading

1. Navigate to **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Review what features will be lost
5. Confirm the downgrade
6. The change takes effect at the end of the current billing period

## Payment

- Payments are processed through **PayPal**
- Supported: PayPal balance, credit/debit cards via PayPal
- Invoices are generated for each payment

### Payment Success

After successful payment:
- You are redirected to the **Payment Success** page
- Your subscription is activated immediately
- A confirmation email is sent

### Payment Failed

If payment fails:
- You are redirected to the **Payment Failed** page
- Your current plan remains active
- Retry payment from the Subscription page

## Usage Limits

The system enforces limits based on your plan:

| Limit | What Happens |
|-------|-------------|
| **Max Users** | Cannot add more users until upgrade |
| **Max Products** | Cannot create new products until upgrade |
| **Max Transactions** | New sales blocked until upgrade or next billing cycle |
| **Max Branches** | Cannot add more branches |

When approaching a limit, a warning appears on the dashboard and relevant pages.

## Subscription Status Bar

A status bar appears at the top when:
- Your subscription is expiring soon
- You've exceeded usage limits
- Payment has failed
- You're on a trial period

## Managing Billing

1. Navigate to **Subscription**
2. View billing history
3. Download invoices
4. Update payment method via PayPal
